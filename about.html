About Me

As an IT student

I am an aspiring young designer with a growing foundation in front-end and back-end development. While I did not start with a passion for tech, I found my drive through design. My focus is on building responsive websites with clean design and simple user flows. I am committed to improving my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, PHP, and MySQL. I aim to grow professionally into a full-stack role, while continuing to strengthen my UX/UI skills and expand into multimedia design. My goal is to create visually unique and meaningful designs that reflect both creativity and purpose.
